What is Struggle & Scran?

Struggle & Scran is a student food helper for people who are hungry, broke, busy, or all three. Instead of buying random ingredients and wasting food, you use what you already have.

The goal is simple: upload your shelfie, choose your diet and cooking time, and get a recipe that actually makes sense for student life.
